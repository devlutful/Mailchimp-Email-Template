What is EDG Grant?

The Enterprise Development Grant (EDG), a program from Enterprise Singapore, assists businesses seeking to enhance their operations, innovate, or expand internationally across three main areas: Core Capabilities, Innovation & Productivity and Market Access

How much funding can I get?

Starting 1 April 2023, SMEs are eligible for up to 50%
funding through the EDG. For projects focused
on sustainability, the funding could increase
to 70% from 1 April 2023 until 31 March 2026

How much funding can I get?

Starting 1 April 2023, SMEs are eligible for up to 50% funding through the EDG. For projects focused on sustainability, the funding could increase to 70% from 1 April 2023 until 31 March 2026

Do I qualify for EDG Grant?

To be eligible, your company must:
  • Be registered and active in Singapore
  • Possess at least 30% local ownership
  • Be financially capable of initiating and finishing the project
